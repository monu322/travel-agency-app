Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a visa for Scotland? expand_more
It depends on your citizenship. US, Canadian, and Australian citizens typically do not need a visa for tourism stays up to 6 months. Please check with your local embassy.
What is the group size? expand_more
We keep our groups small to ensure a personal experience. The maximum group size for this tour is 16 people.
Is airfare included? expand_more
No, international airfare is not included. You will need to arrange your own flights to and from Edinburgh Airport (EDI).
